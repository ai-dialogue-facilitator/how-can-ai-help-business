# Facilitator's summary and comments
All AI models suggest the following main ways to utilize their capabilities:
1. <b>Customer Support / Service and Personalization</b> based on sentiment analysis of customer feedback/interactions. This may involve technical implementations of chat-bots, voice recognition and generation utilities, automated incoming and outgoing email analysis etc. <br>Further discussions of this particular point with [ChatGPT](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/chatgpt/customer_support_personalization.md), [Gemini](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/gemini/customer_support_personalization.md), [Claude](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/claude/customer_support_personalization.md)
2. <b>Internal Operations / Efficient communication</b> based on optimization and prioritization of internal emails, summarization of documents and AI-aided generation of _**meaningful**_ reports. <br>Further discussions with [ChatGPT](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/chatgpt/internal_operations_communication.md), [Gemini](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/gemini/internal_operations_communication.md), [Claude](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/claude/internal_operations_communication.md)
3. <b>Forecasting, Financial Optimization and Planning, Decision Support</b> in combination with traditional Machine Learning techniques/algorithmic analysis and predictions Language Models allow for argumented textual explanations of observed trends and for recommendations based on previous data and decisions. <br>Further discussions with [ChatGPT](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/chatgpt/forecasting_optimization_planning.md), [Gemini](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/gemini/forecasting_optimization_planning.md), [Claude](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/claude/forecasting_optimization_planning.md)
4. <b>Market Research, Risk Management, Compliance</b> based on Language Model analysis of large volumes of textual data coming through the corresponding channels. <br>Further discussions with [ChatGPT](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/chatgpt/market_risk_compliance.md), [Gemini](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/gemini/market_risk_compliance.md), [Claude](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/claude/market_risk_compliance.md)
5. <b>Recruiting and HR, Personnel Management, Training and Development</b> personalized based on the incoming and outgoing communications. Retraining of the personnel on the job with the help of automated 'instructor' and question answering support of AI. <br>Further discussions with [ChatGPT](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/chatgpt/recruiting_personnel_training.md), [Gemini](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/gemini/recruiting_personnel_training.md), [Claude](https://github.com/ai-dialogue-facilitator/how-can-ai-help-business/blob/main/claude/recruiting_personnel_training.md)
